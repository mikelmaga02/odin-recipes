Chocolate brownie recipe

Home page

Description

This is another easy and enjoyable recipe, where I avoid using butter and use olive oil instead. The sugar content is up to the cook, I tend to go on the low side because I enjoy the warm brownie with a nice cold vanilla ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cups flour
  • 2/3 cups cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 3/4 teaspoons sea salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 6 walnuts, crushed

Preparation

  1. Pour the dry ingredients into a bowl and mix.
  2. Pour wet ingredients and mix well.
  3. Heat oven to 160 degrees Celsius
  4. Bake for 40 minutes to an hour, time will vary depending on the size of container.
