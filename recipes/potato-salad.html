Potato salad recipe

Description

This potato salad recipe, Spanish style, is a perfect dish for the hot summer days, as we enjoy it cold. Plus, it is super filling and has a great flavour!

Ingredients

  • 1kg potatoes
  • 450g tuna, in a jar or can
  • 4 large eggs
  • Salt
  • Mayonnaise
  • Olives
  • 350g peas, optional
  • 600g carrots, optional

Steps

  1. Peel potatoes (and carrots), and chop them into small dices for faster cooking time
  2. Bring water to a boil in a large saucepan, and toss potatoes--cook for 15 minutes or until softened.
  3. In a different saucepan, bring water to the boil and cook carrots for 15-20 minutes.
  4. In a small saucepan, bring water to the boil and cook peas for 10 minutes.
  5. Finally, boil the eggs for 7-8 minutes.
  6. Drain and mash the potatoes, then add to a large bowl.
  7. Add the olives and rest of the vegetables to the bowl (if using them).
  8. Add the tuna to the bowl.
  9. Add the mayo to the bowl and combine, add more mayo as needed to ensure a smooth texture.
  10. Add salt to taste.
